A digital heist!

Two teams of hackers are on a mission to steal a rare and valuable ion power from a global security system. Hack into this system via terminal points hidden in the world around you. Each breach recharges your device, steals small amounts of power, and leaks intel about the user identities of your teammates and enemies.

After determining friend from foe, hack your opposing team to steal their power and surcharge your cyberattacks. Keep up a successful streak and watch as your team’s power level increases, while your opponents’ goes down!

Who will emerge the strongest
by the time the system shuts down?
